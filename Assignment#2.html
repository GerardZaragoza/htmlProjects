A short History of Magic:the Gathering

Magic:the Gathering was created by Richard Garfield PhD. during the 90's and was produced by Wizards of the coast. The game is a table top collectible card game where players role play as wizards or planeswakers. two or more players battle it out in the game by casting spells, summoning creatures and playing artifacts as depictedin the cards. The very first release was in 1993, the first set cas called the Alpha edition. It was first shown in the Origins Fair in Dallas, Texas and it became an instant hit among the people who attended the fair. The game can be played both in cards or online. The first MTG online version called Magic Online started around the year 2002 and then came MTG Arena that came out in 2019.
