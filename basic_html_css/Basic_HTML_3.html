The Toronto EDH Club

The Toronto EDH Club is a card gaming club that focuses on "Elder Dragon Highlander" game format or commonly Known as "Commander" format in Magic:The Gathering.
The purpose of this group is to gather like-minded individuals who loves to play Magic:The Gathering and share strategies, battling it out and buy/sell and trade cards.
The members of this group are mostly or if not from Toronto. Although it is Called an EDH club the group also plays other MTG formats like Standard, Modern, Legacy and Vintage and other formats available in MTG
