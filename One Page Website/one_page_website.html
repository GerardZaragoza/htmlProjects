A Short History of Magic: The Gathering

  Magic:the Gathering was created by Richard Garfield PhD. during the 90's and was produced by Wizards of the coast.The game is a table top collectible card game where players role play as wizards or planeswakers. Two or more players battle it out in the game by casting spells, summoning creatures and playing artifacts as depicted in the cards.The very first release was in 1993, the first set was called the Alpha edition. It was first shown in the Origins Fair in Dallas, Texas and it became an instant hit among the people who attended the fair. The game can be played both in cards or online. The first MTG online version called Magic Online started around the year 2002 and then came MTG Arena that came out in 2019.

The basics of Magic: The Gathering

Magic: The Gathering is a game that makes you play as a Wizard or Planeswalkers. As a wizard or planeswalker you have to utilize certain spells that uses the five elements or the five colors of magic.
there are 5 colors in magic that makes you use of spells connected to their colors. the five colors areWhite that represents healing and protection, another is Black that represents death and corruption, the next one is Red which represent fire and destruction, another is Green which represents life and nature, and the last one is Blue which represents the sea and storms.
